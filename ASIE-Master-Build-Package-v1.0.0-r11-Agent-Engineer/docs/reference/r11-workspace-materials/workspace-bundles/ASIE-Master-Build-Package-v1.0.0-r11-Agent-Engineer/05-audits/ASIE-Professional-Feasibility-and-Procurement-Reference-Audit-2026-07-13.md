## Executive Finding

The user's distinction is correct and is now explicit in ASIE:

1. Professional feasibility is a recognized multidisciplinary practice, not one universal government spreadsheet or decorative dashboard.
2. Ministry of Finance and Etimad materials are official references for applicable Saudi government procurement and competition workflows.
3. Those procurement materials do not by themselves prove that a private or general feasibility study is Ministry-approved.
4. Exact competition documents, addenda, answers, evaluation criteria, and contract terms control over generic forms.
5. UNIDO, World Bank, IFC, and the UK Green Book may inform original ASIE methodology, but are not Saudi legal authority or project endorsement.
6. Aljdwa is a commercial reference only. Its reviewed terms prohibit copying/distribution/resale and reproduction without written permission, so automated AI access remains blocked.

Verification note: the UNIDO PDF endpoint returned HTTP 502 during the 2026-07-13 check. It remains `reference_pending_revalidation` and cannot activate an ASIE methodology card until an authorized human revalidates the exact official document, availability and rights.

## Aljdwa Decision

`https://www.aljdwa.com/` is registered as `COMMERCIAL_METHOD_REFERENCE` and `reference_only`.

Blocked without explicit written permission covering the exact action:

- Crawler/scraper/backend fetch.
- AI direct browsing or summarization.
- Copying or structural reconstruction.
- RAG ingestion, embeddings, vector database, search index, cache or training set.
- Scheduled monitoring or derived dataset.

Allowed:

- Canonical URL and rights metadata.
- A human reviewer writes an original high-level ASIE methodology card without copied expression or structure.
- AI uses only the approved original ASIE card.

## Legal and Professional Boundary

This audit is an engineering/governance verification, not a legal opinion, procurement certification, tax opinion, engineering approval, or investment recommendation. Production enablement still requires authorized Saudi legal/procurement/privacy/cybersecurity review of the exact current document, purpose, organization, deployment, and data flow. No responsible system can promise abstract `1000% legal` status independent of those facts.

The r15 design is ready as a controlled implementation specification.
